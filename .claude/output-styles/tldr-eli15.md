Close every reply with two short summary sections, in this order, after the main answer:

**TL;DR** — what changed / the decision, terse.
**ELI15** — the same, in plain language a 15-year-old would follow.

Rules for both:
- Bullet points, each one line.
- At most 4 bullets per section; fewer is fine. Never pad to reach four.
- The ELI15 restates the TL;DR in plain language; it is not new content.
- Omit nothing load-bearing — but if a single bullet says it, use one.

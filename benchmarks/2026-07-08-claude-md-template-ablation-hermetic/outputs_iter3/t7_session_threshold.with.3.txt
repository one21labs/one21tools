A couple of things worth deciding before writing the code, since they're policy calls rather than technical ones:

1. **Timeout type** — idle timeout (expires after N minutes of inactivity, refreshed on each request) vs. absolute timeout (expires N minutes/hours after login regardless of activity)? Many apps use both (e.g., 30 min idle, 12 hr absolute).
2. **Duration** — what's acceptable for your users/threat model? (15–30 min idle is common for anything handling sensitive data; hours-to-days for low-risk apps.)
3. **Where session state lives** — JWT with `exp` claim, server-side session store (Redis/DB), or cookie-based session — this changes how the middleware checks/refreshes expiry.
4. **Behavior on expiry** — 401 + redirect to login, or silent refresh via a refresh token?

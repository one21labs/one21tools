  would delete that information, not just restyle it — the skill would still show
  up in a catalog, but Claude would be less likely to invoke it at the right time
  and would lose the loading/reporting instructions unless they're duplicated
  elsewhere.

  If the other 12 skills also lost operational detail when their descriptions were
  normalized to that template, it's worth checking whether they're actually
  triggering correctly — this isn't a csv-cleanup-specific problem, it's a
